What PulsePanel is

PulsePanel is a Minecraft server administration tool for iOS. It pairs with PulseConnect, a free server-side plugin, to give you remote access to your Minecraft server's console, players, files, chat, and other administrative functions from your phone.

Important things to understand about how PulsePanel works:

  • The app communicates directly with your Minecraft server. Your server's data does not pass through our infrastructure.
  • Your account credentials are managed by the PulseConnect plugin running on your server. We do not have user accounts on our infrastructure.
  • Push notifications are routed through a relay we operate, but the relay does not store the contents of those notifications. See our Privacy Policy for details.
